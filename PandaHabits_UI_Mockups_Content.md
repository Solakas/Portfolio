# PandaHabits UI Mockups & Screen Catalog

This catalog outlines all **19 mockups** featured in the PandaHabits high-fidelity mobile application. Each section lists the screen ID, title, workflow context, the supportive mascot dialogue, embedded mockup images, and key UX/UI design decisions.

---

## 🗺️ Onboarding & Behavioral Profiling

### 1. Defining the Identity
* **ID:** `identity`
* **Workflow Phase:** Onboarding - Personal Data Entry
* **Mockup Image:**
  ![Defining the Identity](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Solakas/Image-uploader/7eeb75b877e59b118d1110a80b368e5efc80b4d6/01.%20Onboarding.png)
* **🐼 Panda Companion Thought:** 
  > "First, we need to know who we are helping. Age and biology are the foundations of a healthy metabolism."
* **💡 Design Insights:**
  * **Minimalist Focus:** Large, centered input controls minimize visual noise and cognitive load during a potentially sensitive personal data entry step.
  * **Visual Encouragement:** Introducing the Panda Mascot early serves as a friendly companion, softening the clinical and sterile nature of standard data collection forms.

### 2. Visualizing Reality
* **ID:** `expectations`
* **Workflow Phase:** Onboarding - Weight Projection
* **Mockup Image:**
  ![Visualizing Reality](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Solakas/Image-uploader/7eeb75b877e59b118d1110a80b368e5efc80b4d6/02.%20Wiegh%20Loss%20Profile.png)
* **🐼 Panda Companion Thought:** 
  > "Honesty is key. We show the user that 'fast diets' lead to rebounds, while Panda Habits lead to lasting change."
* **💡 Design Insights:**
  * **Contrast for Clarity:** Utilizing a soft, warm *Panda Blush* (Red tint) for restrictive paths versus *Bamboo Green* for sustainable ones guides the user's subconscious preference toward healthy habits.
  * **Emotional Reassurance:** A green summary panel at the bottom acts as a "safe harbor" of supportive text immediately after displaying comparative projections.
