# 03.2 High Fidelity Mockups

Phase: 03. Ideate

## Set the stage

<aside>
❓

**Purpose**
To synthesize our analytical briefs, wireframes, and design system into the final, pixel-perfect visual representations of the "Apply Now" platform. This stage transforms the structural blueprints into the actual product interface that the end-users (our HR personas) will interact with daily.

</aside>

<aside>
🏗️

**The Process
We** will use Figma to assemble the screens (Candidates List and Candidate Info page) strictly leveraging the pre-built, code-ready components from our newly established **Sol Design System**. By utilizing Sol's semantic tokens for our elegant blue theme, typography, and spacing, we will piece together the complex data tables, scorecards, and filtering layouts defined in earlier phases.

</aside>

<aside>
🎯

**Goal**
To produce a highly polished, enterprise-grade UI that is visually stunning, deeply intuitive, and completely free of cognitive friction. Because these mockups are built using Sol, the final goal is not just a pretty picture, but a completely developer-ready layout with a true 1:1 parity to React/TypeScript code.

</aside>
