### Draft Persona 3:  (The High-Volume Recruiter)

*Focus: Speed, scannability, and bulk processing on the Candidates list.*

<aside>
💬

**User Story 1 (Filtering):** 

As a High-Volume Recruiter, I want to use persistent filtering functionality to instantly sort the candidates list by dealbreakers like "location" and "right-to-work", so that I don't waste time looking at unqualified applicants.

</aside>

<aside>
💬

**User Story 2 (Scanning):** 

As a High-Volume Recruiter, I want to see the candidate's name and other essential information (like current title and application date) directly on the list view, so that I can quickly assess them without clicking into their full profile

</aside>

<aside>
💬

User Story 3 (Bulk Actions)

As a High-Volume Recruiter, I want to select multiple people from the list with all candidates that applied to a job opening , and perform a bulk action (e.g., Reject or Move to Phone Screen), so that I can process hundreds of applications effortlessly.

</aside>

---

### Draft Persona 2:  (The Technical Recruiter)

*Focus: Deep context, seamless evaluation, and collaboration on the Candidates info page.*

<aside>
💬

**User Story 1 (Effortless Evaluation):** 

As a Technical Recruiter, I want to view the candidate's actual resume inline alongside my evaluation workspace on the candidate's info page, so that I can review their skills without downloading a file or switching tabs.

</aside>

<aside>
💬

**User Story 2 (Consolidated Context)):** 

As a Technical Recruiter, I want all related information displayed in one place, including clickable links to external portfolios (like GitHub), so that I have a complete picture of their technical abilities.

</aside>

<aside>
💬

User Story 3 (Collaboration)

As a High-Volume Recruiter, I want to select multiple people from the list with all candidates that applied to a job opening , and perform a bulk action (e.g., Reject or Move to Phone Screen), so that I can process hundreds of applications effortlessly.

</aside>

---

### Draft Persona 3:  (The HR Director)

*Focus: Historical data, advanced search, and keeping resumes for future references.*

<aside>
💬

**User Story 1 (Advanced Search):** 

As an HR Director, I want robust search functionality available on the candidates list, so that I can query our entire database for specific skills when a new role opens up.

</aside>

<aside>
💬

**User Story 2 (Historical Context):** 

As an HR Director, I want the candidate's info page to clearly display their past application history and previous scorecards , so that I can evaluate them with full context based on keeping the resumes for future references.

</aside>

<aside>
💬

User Story 3 (Talent Re-engagement)

As an HR Director, I want to be able to select a past candidate from the candidates list and assign them to a new recruitment campaign, so that we can actively utilize our existing talent pool.

</aside>
