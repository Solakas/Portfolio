## The Core Challenge

The primary challenge was balancing two distinct, often conflicting needs within recruitment platforms:

1. Creating a high-speed, frictionless interface that solves "click fatigue" for bulk candidate processing.
2. Providing a deep, context-rich workspace for collaborative, technical candidate evaluation.

## The Process & The "Two-Round" Interview Strategy

I structured my approach around a standard 5-stage UX methodology (Analyse, Define, Ideate, Test, Measure).

During the initial research phase, I conducted **two distinct rounds of user interviews**. To execute this efficiently and gather diverse data, I first carefully defined the research goals and drafted specific interview scripts. I then leveraged AI to conduct mock interviews, simulating realistic responses from various HR professionals.

## Validating the Design

To truly validate my design decisions, I took the execution a step beyond static Figma mockups to see the interface in action. Using **Claude Code**, I translated the core designs into a functional [React/TypeScript application](https://apply-now-platform-oysw.vercel.app/). The codebase is version-controlled via **GitHub** and deployed live via Vercel.

I chose to build a working, coded prototype because static screens cannot accurately capture the "feel" of a platform. This step allowed me to focus heavily on interaction design—implementing delightful micro-animations and refining the responsiveness of complex data filtering. Most importantly, transitioning to code enabled me to conduct realistic User Acceptance Testing (UAT) and usability testing, which is essential for evaluating complex state management and bulk-action logic in a live browser environment.

As an added bonus to this journey, bridging this gap between design and development ensured that all Figma components are structured to be fully code-generation ready.

## The Final Outcome

Below, you will find the complete documentation of this process. The deliverables include:

- **Research & Architecture:** Empathy maps, defined user flows, and the platform sitemap.
- **UI Guidelines:** The foundational rules and component behaviors that keep the interface consistent.
- **High-Fidelity Mockups:** Detailed analytical briefs and visuals for the Candidates List and Evaluation views.
- **Working Prototype:** A live, interactive React deployment demonstrating real-time filtering and interaction design.
- **Measurement Strategy:** A proposed post-launch analytics framework to continuously validate the UX decisions.
